Can we imagine an internet that respects our digital rights?

The importance of strong data protection policies cannot be understated. In the absence of strong, proactive legislation, internet users around the world remain vulnerable to intentional attacks on their privacy.

Making sense of the data privacy landscape

The Advocating for Data Accountability, Protection, and Transparency (ADAPT), a project of Internews, is building a coordinated advocacy effort to promote rights-respecting privacy policies and help internet users understand what protections currently exist to keep their data, and themselves, safe online.

Informed advocacy

Data protection laws and regulations are complex and evolving. We equip traditional internet rights advocates with the legal resources and expertise to conduct informed and effective campaigns.
