Privacy is global podcast

Privacidad, siri, privacy, privacidade. Finding the word in different languages might look simple. The contexts in which privacy plays out around the world, however, is definitely not. What does privacy mean for people in countries around the world? How are new technologies and widespread data aggregation impacting societies, politics, and cultures? What are the stories of the activists working to protect privacy rights and advocate for adequate laws, regulations and practices to ensure data is collected, processed, analyzed and used responsibly?

Listen to ‘Privacy is Global’, a podcast brought to you by Internews, to learn more about the context, the struggles and the people behind data protection around the world!
